The Silent Poet

Here he comes, the silent poet
Walking as is he was dead
Dragging his sorrows
No one hears him, except the silent itself

Here he comes, the weak poet trying to speak
He tries and tries, but he can't
He writes to express himself

He tries to speak again, but again he can't
He gives up, until he remembers
He doesn't need to speak, because he can write
He leaves the town with hope in his heart, and a poem in his mind.
